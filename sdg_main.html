SDG one - No Poverty

SDG 1 aims to eradicate poverty globally by addressing its multidimensional aspects, including income inequality, social exclusion, and lack of access to basic services, to ensure that all people can lead a life of dignity and opportunity.

SDG two - Zero Hunger

SDG 2 focuses on ending hunger, achieving food security, improving nutrition, and promoting sustainable agriculture by supporting small-scale farmers, investing in agricultural research and infrastructure, and ensuring equitable access to food for all.
